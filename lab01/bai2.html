Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my fav foods. Here is a recipe for delicously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter with a frying pan.
  2. Mix the eggs with cream.
  3. Once butter had been melted add cream and eggs.
  4. Fold the eggs, scroll or maybe make it in the you want.
  5. Take your spoon and enjoy.
